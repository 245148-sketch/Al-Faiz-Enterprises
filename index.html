Who We Are

AL-FAIZ Enterprises has provided a vital link for Pakistan businesses requiring transportation of consignments within Pakistan. We have built a reputation of reliability in transportation of goods locally and nationally.

Our success is due to our professional approach, competitive pricing and large network. With the quality of service rendered by us right from the beginning, we have become a trusted name in Pakistan's logistics industry.

We pride ourselves on our high standards of ethical business conduct and exceptional service levels. Our objective is quality — to improve service levels and always be prepared to initiate change to meet our clients' requirements.
